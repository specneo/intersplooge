Sweet 'n' Cinnamon PopCorn

Ingredients

  • Popcorn Kernels 200g
  • Oil 2 Tablespoons
  • Cinnamon 3 Teaspoons
  • Caster Sugar
  • Butter 1 Tablesoon

Method

  1. Grab a stainless steel pan, place on a med/high heat
  2. Place the lid on top of the pan and wait for that first 'pop'
  3. mix together cinnamon and sugar in seperate bowl
  4. Turn the heat down to 'medium', keep waiting 'till the pop bout to stop'
  5. Add the butter, keep stirring until evenly coated
  6. Slowly decant sugar/ cinnamon mixture into popcorn pan, whilst maintaining consistent movement within the pan to ensure even coating
  7. Wack a film on, serve up an enjoy
